ChMe Token

Promotional Token

Primary Purpose

ChMe rewards users for spreading awareness and driving community growth. Think of it as 'proof of promotion' — as our visibility grows, the more USDC everyone can earn.

Exchange Model

ChMe introduces a revolutionary dual-exchange engine.

Following your gains of $ChMe, our team will allocate and lock a portion of USDC into a smart contract to build the reward pool.

Holders will then face a strategic choice: swap for the platform token $Ch , or wait for the locked $USDC pool.

As more users choose to swap for $Ch, the total circulating supply of $ChMe shrinks, which mathematically drives up the USDC exchange value per remaining token.

When the USDC pool officially opens, the exchange rate will be determined strictly by math: the total USDC in the pool divided by the remaining circulating supply of $ChMe

Product Story

Imagine you're scrolling on your phone and find a great video. You want to see the whole thing, so you go on Cheaply and pay a little USDC to unlock the full video — just like OnlyFans.

1. As soon as you pay, you get the video and get the creator's special token. The money you paid is split: one part goes to the creator as profit, and the other part goes into a secure system "vault" as a backup fund.

2. You are fully in control of these tokens. You can keep them, or you can give them back to the "vault" anytime to get USDC back.

3. As the video goes viral, more fans get these tokens. Real-world shops like clothing stores notice this group of fans with tokens. To get their business, these shops let fans buy clothes and food with the tokens and premium perks.

4. Why would a shop accept tokens? Because the system's "vault" backs them up. Shop owners can exchange the tokens for USDC. They don't worry about the token going to "Zero", and users get to spend their tokens on real-world stuff.

5. As more shops join, they want to stand out. To advertise, some shops start adding their own USDC into the system's vault as a marketing expense. When the vault has more money, tokens become worth more USDC! This makes more people want the tokens, which brings in more shops, creating a winning cycle for everyone.
