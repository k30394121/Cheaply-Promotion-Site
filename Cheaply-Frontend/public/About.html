Promo Game

Gamified Marketing Platform

What It Is

Token Tycoon is an interactive promotion platform that merges "Lucky Wheel" mechanics with "Fluctuating Ticket Trading". Players earn game tokens through intuitive wheel spins and engage in lottery competitions featuring market-driven volatility. By simulating the thrills of financial markets, it transforms platform engagement into a strategic gaming experience.

How It Works

Spin to Win: Every account receives 5 free spins upon registration and one free weekly spin to win high-value game tokens on a fixed 8-slot wheel.

Card Buffs: Unlock "Basic Achievement" or "Advanced Luck" cards by completing specific actions—such as consecutive logins or token purchases—to gain prize multipliers or extra spin opportunities.

Lottery Arena (Futures Trading): Use accumulated tokens to purchase "Dynamic Price Tickets". All spent tokens enter a prize pool, where a single winner can claim the entire pot, or the pool carries over if no one wins.

Token Conversion: Purchasing Web2 game tokens grants immediate bonuses of Web3 promotional tokens at a fixed ratio , capped at 5,000 promo tokens per user.
